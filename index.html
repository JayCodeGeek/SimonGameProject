Simon Game

Instructions

Before playing it's better to know the rules

- This game is all about selecting the right sequence of colors.

- Click on play button so that out of four any one color box is flashed automatically.

- Everytime user clicks on play button the color sequence gets stored.

- User can then click on color boxes in sequence and check if the selected sequence of colors are right or wrong by clicking on check button.

- Inorder to restart, user has to click on restart button.
